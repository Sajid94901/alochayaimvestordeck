4) Human Resources Management (HRM) & Culture Plan

Our HRM strategy is designed to build a loyal, highly-skilled team that embodies the spirit of Alo Chaya, turning service into an art form. Our core principle is: "We are hosts, not staff."

A. Recruitment & Onboarding

  • Local First Initiative: We will prioritize hiring from the local Chandpur community, creating sustainable employment and fostering strong local ties.
  • The Alo Chaya Immersion: Every new hire will undergo a 7-day onboarding program personally led by my father, the Founder. This is not just training; it is an induction into our culture and the core principles of guest obsession.

B. Training, Development & Dignity

  • Culinary Training Led by Operation Lead: My $15+$ years of self-taught culinary expertise is our greatest competitive advantage and will form the core of our training.
  • Defined Career Paths: We have established clear, dignified promotion paths for all roles (e.g., Commis Chef to Sous-Artisan, Garçon to Service Captain), proving we are a career destination, not just a job. This is key to retaining top talent.

5) Operations Plan: The Guest Journey

My operational plan ensures a flawless, memorable, and repeatable luxury experience for every guest, founded on the principles of holistic well-being.

A. The Wellness Center: Our "Brand Moat"

The Wellness Center is the physical manifestation of our core philosophy. It is a sanctuary where guests can embark on their own journey of well-being, focusing on the 8 Dimensions of Wellness grounded in Islamic principles. This is not an amenity; it is our unique "Brand Moat" that cannot be copied.

B. Service Standards & Quality Control

  • The "HEART" Service Recovery Model: All staff will be trained in our simple but powerful model for turning any issue into a positive experience: Hear, Empathize, Apologize, Resolve, Thank.
  • The Daily Morning Briefing: Each day, the management team will review all guest feedback from the previous 24 hours to identify and act on areas for immediate improvement, creating a culture of constant refinement.
