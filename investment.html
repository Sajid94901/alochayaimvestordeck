Table 4: HR Policies, Principles & Doctrine

Our static, investor-facing HR doctrine, tying people strategy to brand, KPIs, and leadership development.

Policy Area Doctrine & Applied Approach
Core Philosophy “Dignified Livelihoods & Holistic Well-Being.” — We pay dignity wages, ensure predictable payroll, provide staff wellness access, and build clear career paths. Every HR policy maps directly to our brand identity and the guest experience.
Hiring & Assessment Multi-stage selection: CV → phone screen → role simulation / stress interview → short written scenario → verbal presentation → references. We use Maslow-informed screening for motivation and stability, with objective scoring and a 3-month probation period.
Leadership Model Suite We embed multiple complementary leadership frameworks to shape development and succession:
  • Jim Collins (Level 5): We promote leaders who combine deep humility with intense professional will.
  • John C. Maxwell (5 Levels): We craft development ladders tied to compensation and equity vesting based on a manager's progression.
  • Kelley (Followership): We cultivate "effective" followers who think critically and act in alignment with our strategy.
  • Situational Leadership: Managers are trained to flex between directing, coaching, supporting, and delegating based on employee needs.
Personality & Psychometrics We use science-backed personality screening for optimal role-fit:
  • NEO / Big Five (OCEAN): A baseline measure of Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism.
  • DeYoung & Peterson Facets: For senior roles, we use deeper facet mapping (e.g., self-discipline, warmth, achievement-striving).

Usage: Applied as a baseline, not a sole criterion, and combined with role-performance simulations.
Assessment & Promotion A short assessment center for managerial posts includes leaderless group tasks, guest recovery role-plays, and operational case studies to map candidates against our leadership models. Promotion is gated by demonstrated followership and leadership qualities.
Governance & Brand Alignment All policies are codified in our Employee Handbook & HRIS. Every HR metric—from recruitment language to service scripts—maps directly to our Brand Identity (Kapferer) and Brand Performance (Keller) models. Our staff are our live brand ambassadors.
Project Lead Contingency In Worst & Improbable scenarios, the Project Lead contributes 50% of monthly salary to a contingency equity pool until a pre-agreed breakeven KPI is met, ensuring full skin-in-the-game for investors.
