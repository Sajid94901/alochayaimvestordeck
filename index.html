The Vision: An Expansive & Luxurious Retreat

Alo Chaya Limited is an 87-bigha tropical resort on the banks of the Meghna River, blending natural serenity with high-end amenities. Our design emphasizes a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces, using natural materials like bamboo and teak to create an eco-friendly and culturally authentic atmosphere just a 75km drive from the heart of Dhaka.

Detailed Features & Amenities

Riverfront & Water Activities

A long, meandering walkway follows the contours of the Meghna River bank, offering tranquil spots for guests to enjoy breathtaking sunrises and sunsets. An expansive, private water body is dedicated to game fishing, stocked with local species and featuring a rustic wooden boathouse.

Gardens & Recreation

A Grand Lawn offers panoramic river views for large events, while a more intimate Garden Lawn is bordered by extensive tropical fruit and flower gardens. Guests can explore an orchard of indigenous fruit trees, with seasonal harvests served fresh in our restaurants.

Accommodations & Dining

The 60,000 sq. ft. residential facility offers premium lodging across three stories. A world-class commercial kitchen provides high-end culinary service. Future plans include an open-air main restaurant with stunning river vistas and smaller, themed eateries tucked away in scenic spots.

Sustainability & Design

The architecture predominantly uses locally sourced, natural materials. Design elements like open-air concepts and large windows are utilized for passive cooling, minimizing environmental impact and blending the resort seamlessly into its natural surroundings.
