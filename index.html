Heritage & Asset Foundation

Paternal Legacy: The 100-Year Consolidation

The project is secured by a powerful generational cycle, establishing the family's financial security in the city and on-site.

  • **The Departure:** My paternal great-grandfather (**The Daroga**) left the original riverine land in **Enayet Nagar**.
  • **The City Foundation:** He built the **Fatima Mahal** in **Sadiq Bazar**.
  • **The Bungalow (2009):** My father (**The Founder**) built the **5-room bungalow** ($50\text{m} \times 32\text{m}$ footprint) on the riverine land, marking the first large return to the site.
  • **The Collateral Asset (2016-2017):** My father built the residential/commercial **Mahmud Tower** on the **Fatima Mahal** land. This **$116$ Crore BDT** asset serves as primary collateral.

Maternal Roots & Professional Management

The investment is managed by the next generation, rooted in century-old local trust and professional planning.

  • **Sarker Bari Legacy (1912):** My maternal great-grandfather purchased the **Sarkar Bari** land near **Matlab, Chadpur**, establishing a century-long local presence.
  • **Local Anchor:** This continuity, reinforced by my maternal grandfather's family, ensures deep community trust for the resort.
  • **My Activation (2023):** As Operation Lead, I initiated professional planning, engaging **SGS Bangladesh** and **NSU Startynext** for system verification.
  • **Commitment:** I am personally reinvesting 50% of my salary to guarantee operational stability.

Tangible Assets & Security

The investment is secured against wholly-owned family assets, eliminating the major risks of construction and land acquisition.

  • **Primary Collateral:** The city-center **Mahmud Tower** is valued at **$116$ Crore BDT**.
  • **Project Asset:** The **Alo Chaya Resort Building** itself is **$60,000$ sq ft** across $4$ floors, valued at **$60$ Crore BDT**.
  • **Land Base:** **85 Bighas** (or **20.23 Kani**) of wholly-owned family land, including the riverine resort plot.
