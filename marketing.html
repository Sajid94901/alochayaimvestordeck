The Customer Journey: From Salience to Resonance (Keller's CBBE)

Our 4-Step Brand Building Funnel

Step 1: Identity (Salience)

Goal: Make the brand instantly recognizable as the premier **Riverine Wellness Resort** near Dhaka.

Action: Consistent, high-quality visual identity and location-based digital ads.

Step 2: Meaning (Performance & Imagery)

Goal: Deliver superior **Performance** (cleanliness, reliable service) and powerful **Imagery** (cultural authenticity, spiritual peace).

Action: Influencer marketing focused on **experience** over amenities. Staff training (HEART Model).

Step 3: Response (Judgments & Feelings)

Goal: Elicit **Judgments** of Quality and Credibility, and **Feelings** of Warmth, Security, and Self-Respect.

Action: Proactive guest feedback loop, showcase 5-star reviews on all platforms, highlight staff professionalism.

Step 4: Resonance (Relationships) - THE MOAT

Goal: Achieve **Behavioral Loyalty** and **Sense of Community** to create the competitive moat (high repeat rate).

Action: Cultural immersion programs, exclusive loyalty club (Alo Chaya Circle), and active brand engagement online.

Marketing Strategies: Attack & Defense

Frontal Attack (The Direct Fight)

Match competitor amenities and directly highlight our advantage in **size (17.5 acres)** and **natural setting**.

Flank Attack (The Niche)

Target underserved **weekend getaway & local urban markets** seeking structured cultural/wellness experiences.

Bypass Strategy (The New Market)

Promote unique experiences unavailable in urban hotels: **river tours, cultural workshops, and holistic wellness** (our main POD).

Guerrilla Warfare (The Buzz)

Pop-up cultural experiences, micro-influencer visits, and immersive digital storytelling to achieve high **CBBE Resonance** quickly.
